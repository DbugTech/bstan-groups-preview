About Bstan Cleaning

BBSTAN CLEANING SERVICES is one of the leading home service company in Nigeria, a company created with the sole purpose of rendering services to corporate organizations and Industries in the areas of providing quality janitorial/housekeeping services.

We effectively handle facade cleaning, fumigation/pest control, Waste disposals (solid & liquid), Restoration of floors, Mechanized tree trimming, Landscaping, etc.

Our services are designed to meet all classes of business needs to suit the desire and budget of our Clients
