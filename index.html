About Bstan Group

We are passionate and provide affordable Solutions for all

We Provide Personal and Corporate Housing Solutions and making buying and selling of real estate as cost effective as possible while maintaining the highest level of service. We provide accurate and up-to-date information, skilled analysis and sound real estate advice. We continually explore new ideas and technology, to make the selling and buying of real estate faster, less costly, and easier.
At Bstan Homes we are one of the foremost leading real estate and homeowner service company from Africa, always exceeding our customers’ expectations. As we provide the most professional, informative, loyal and dedicated service in the industry. The best interests of our clients will always come first and we will place the clients’ concerns ahead of our own in each and every transaction, as we are dedicated to the development of long-term client relationships! Our team-approach philosophy ensures your needs are important to each and every member of our organization.
